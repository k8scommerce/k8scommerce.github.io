What is K8sCommerce?

K8sCommerce is a Go native, Kubernetes native, and Earth friendly ecommerce platform with a shared focus of being highly performant and ESG compliant.

Notebooks

K8sCommerce includes services to create and manage interactive Jupyter notebooks. You can customize your notebook deployment and your compute resources to suit your data science needs. Experiment with your workflows locally, then deploy them to a cloud when you're ready.

TensorFlow model training

K8sCommerce provides a custom TensorFlow training job operator that you can use to train your ML model. In particular, K8sCommerce's job operator can handle distributed TensorFlow training jobs. Configure the training controller to use CPUs or GPUs and to suit various cluster sizes.

Model serving

K8sCommerce supports a TensorFlow Serving container to export trained TensorFlow models to Kubernetes. K8sCommerce is also integrated with Seldon Core, an open source platform for deploying machine learning models on Kubernetes, NVIDIA Triton Inference Server for maximized GPU utilization when deploying ML/DL models at scale, and MLRun Serving, an open-source serverless framework for deployment and monitoring of real-time ML/DL pipelines.


Pipelines

K8sCommerce Pipelines is a comprehensive solution for deploying and managing end-to-end ML workflows. Use K8sCommerce Pipelines for rapid and reliable experimentation. You can schedule and compare runs, and examine detailed reports on each run.

Multi-framework

Our development plans extend beyond TensorFlow. We're working hard to extend the support of PyTorch, Apache MXNet, MPI, XGBoost, Chainer, and more. We also integrate with Istio and Ambassador for ingress, Nuclio as a fast multi-purpose serverless framework, and Pachyderm for managing your data science pipelines.
